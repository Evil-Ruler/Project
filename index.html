Our Coffee Story

From bean to brew, discover the passion and craftsmanship behind every cup of BrewAura coffee. Our journey is about more than just coffee—it's about community, sustainability, and the perfect cup.

Scroll to Explore
2010

Our Humble Beginnings

BrewAura began as a small cart in the corner of a local farmers market. Founder Emma Chen, dissatisfied with corporate life, decided to pursue her passion for coffee and share it with others.

Using a single-origin bean from Colombia and a portable espresso machine, Emma served her first customers with a smile and a commitment to quality that would become the foundation of our brand.

2013

First Brick & Mortar

After three years of growth and a loyal following, BrewAura opened its first permanent location in a historic building downtown. The café quickly became known for its warm atmosphere and expertly crafted beverages.

This milestone marked our expansion into food offerings, with locally sourced pastries and a small breakfast menu that complemented our signature coffee drinks.

2016

Direct Trade Partnerships

Committed to sustainability and ethical sourcing, we established direct relationships with coffee farmers in Colombia, Ethiopia, and Guatemala. These partnerships ensure fair compensation and environmentally responsible farming practices.

Emma and our head roaster began traveling annually to visit our partner farms, deepening our understanding of the craft and bringing back new techniques and varietals to share with our customers.

2019

In-House Roastery

A significant leap forward came with the opening of our own roastery, allowing complete control over the coffee development process. This facility enabled us to experiment with different roast profiles and processing methods.

The roastery also opened its doors to the public for tours and tasting sessions, creating an educational space where coffee enthusiasts could deepen their appreciation for the craft.

2023

Today & Beyond

BrewAura has grown to five locations across the city, each with its unique character but united by our commitment to extraordinary coffee experiences. Our team has expanded to include baristas, roasters, and educators who share our passion.

Looking ahead, we're focused on expanding our sustainability initiatives, continuing to innovate our offerings, and creating more opportunities to connect with our community over the perfect cup of coffee.

Our Brewing Process

The journey from bean to cup is a meticulous one, guided by expertise and respect for the craft. Here's how we create the perfect brew.

1

Bean Selection

We carefully select beans from our partner farms, focusing on seasonality and peak flavor profiles.

2

Small-Batch Roasting

Each batch is roasted to perfection, bringing out the unique characteristics of the origin.

3

Precision Grinding

Beans are ground to the exact coarseness required for each brewing method moments before use.

4

Artful Brewing

Our baristas use precise techniques, controlling water temperature and extraction time for optimal flavor.

5

Serving Experience

Each cup is presented with attention to detail, creating a moment of connection and enjoyment.
